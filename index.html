Important caveat

This flow is workable in Asana, but requires simplification versus Airtable's current setup. Custom web-app-like Airtable interfaces — screens that stitch data across multiple bases with bespoke UI — are not something Asana is committing to replicate. The proposed flow uses Asana-native primitives: projects, tasks, custom fields, rules, and table records.
